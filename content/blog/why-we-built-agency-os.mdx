# Why We Built Agency OS

Every creative agency faces the same problem: managing clients, projects, deliverables, and invoices across multiple disconnected tools.

We started Agency OS because we were tired of context-switching. As founders with agency experience, we knew there had to be a better way. Instead of toggling between project management, invoicing, contract storage, and client communication tools, what if everything lived in one place?

## The Problem We Solved

Creative agencies typically use:
- A project management tool (Asana, Monday, Notion)
- Separate invoicing software (Wave, Freshbooks)
- Email or shared drives for contracts
- A client portal (if they even have one)
- Slack or email for communication

This creates friction. Information gets siloed. Teams waste time switching between tools. Clients can't track progress in one place. Invoices get lost in email.

## Our Solution

Agency OS consolidates everything. Your team manages projects and deliverables. Clients see real-time updates in their own portal. Invoices are generated automatically from completed work. Contracts are stored securely. Everything is integrated.

The result? Agencies spend less time on admin and more time doing creative work.
