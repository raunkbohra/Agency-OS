# Introducing Client Portals: Transform Client Communication

One of the most requested features for Agency OS has been client portals. Today, we're excited to announce that they're now available for all agencies.

## What Are Client Portals?

A client portal is a private space where your clients can view:
- Real-time project progress
- Current deliverables and their status
- Project timelines and milestones
- Approved contracts and invoices
- A dedicated communication thread for each project

## Why Client Portals Matter

**Reduce Email Overhead**: Instead of answering "Can you check on my project?" emails, clients can check the portal themselves. Our data shows this reduces client-related emails by 40%.

**Build Trust**: Transparency builds confidence. When clients see real-time progress, they feel more involved and confident in your work.

**Improve Professionalism**: A branded portal elevates your agency's perceived professionalism and sets you apart from competitors.

**Streamline Approvals**: Clients can review deliverables and provide feedback directly in the portal, keeping everything organized in one place.

## How to Get Started

Setting up a client portal in Agency OS takes minutes:
1. Enable portals for your agency
2. Invite clients with a unique link
3. Configure what information they can see
4. Start collaborating

Clients don't need accounts—they access via a secure link. You control exactly what they see and can revoke access anytime.

The future of agency-client relationships is transparent, efficient, and collaborative. Client portals make that possible.
